About

Karaokey is a vocal remover that automatically separates the vocals and instruments. A deep learning model based on LSTMs has been trained to tackle the source separation. The model learns the particularities of music signals through its temporal structure. For more information on how to use it please check this link.

Copyright law for music:

Musical recordings and score are protected under UK copyright law outlined in Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1998. This states that musical recordings are automatically copyrighted whenever an individual or company creates the work (music). This individual or company becomes the ‘first owner of copyright’ under the act.

Under this act, it is an offence to:

  • Copy the music.
  • Rent, lend or issue the music to others,
  • Perform, broadcast or show the music to public.
  • Adapt the music.
  • Copy the music.
